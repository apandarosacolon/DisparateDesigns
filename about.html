About Us

Who we are and what we do

Disparate Designs was founded in the basement of sisters Rebecca and Raven, who spent their weekends making knock-off band tees for their broke friends. Word of mouth helped us grow, and before long, we needed to expand. Today we have a fully operational print and design space, as well as a spacious gallery for local artists to display their work. We specialize in what makes you special! Our services create the most one-of-a-kind products imaginable.
